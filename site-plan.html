Site Name

WanderWorld

The World Through Your Eyes

This name was selected because it represents the idea of exploring ("wander") different places around the world. It is short, memorable, and fits a travel-focused website.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a travel guide that highlights popular tourist destinations, provides useful travel tips, and offers detailed information about specific locations. Users will be able to explore destinations and learn important details before planning a trip.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find affordable travel destinations?
  • What are the must-see attractions in a specific location?
  • What should I pack when traveling internationally?
